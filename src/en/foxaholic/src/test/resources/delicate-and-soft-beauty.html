Summary

Chuchu finds herself transported into a post-apocalyptic progression-style novel.

Her biological brother is the overpowered male protagonist.

Her future sister-in-law has a rare healing ability and is a formidable female lead.

Her father is the base’s strongest ruler.

And she…

She’s truly remarkable – right from the start, she rescues the most formidable villain from the novel, along with an adorable sidekick, making significant contributions to preserving and continuing the light of humanity.

————–

After his rebirth, Chu Lin first called his sister, urging her to seize the final opportunity to stockpile supplies and protect herself.

She must not perish prematurely as she had in his previous life.

What he never anticipated was…

When he finally endured great hardships to find his sister, he didn’t even get a chance to present the meticulously prepared clothes and snacks.

His sister resided in the best room, slept on the finest bed, enjoyed the most exquisite food, and led the most leisurely life. The exceptional man beside her, with striking features, doted on her more excessively than even Chu Lin himself, her own brother!

In their previous lives, this person was his fiercest adversary – moody, ruthless, and a blood-soaked executioner – now behaved restrained and courteously, extending a slightly hesitant hand towards Chu Lin.

“Hello, big brother.”

Chu Lin was utterly speechless.
